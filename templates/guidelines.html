Community Guidelines

Welcome to PLAYed!

In order to keep our community safe and open to anyone, please follow our guidelines.

  1. 1. Be polite and respectful.

  2. 2. Do not share personal and/or private information.

  3. 3. Do not use this platform to spread racist, homophobic, sexist and/or other discriminatory rhetoric.

  4. 4. Personal attacks, abuse, and aggressive content towards other users will not be tolerated.

  5. 5. Feel free to post questions/discussion relevant to PLAYed's content.

Failure to comply with these guidelines may result in post or account deletion.
